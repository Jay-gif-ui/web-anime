Mononogatari Season 2 Adds Hidenori Takahashi as Tenjitsu to Its Cast

The official Twitter account for the anime adaptation of Onigunsō’s Malevolent Spirits: Mononogatari manga has unveiled exciting news. Hidenori Takahashi, who already lends his voice to the character Hayate in both seasons of the anime, will now take on the role of Tenjitsu. The highly-anticipated second season of the series made its debut on July 3, following the successful premiere of the first season on January 9, which aired on Tokyo MX, BS11, and other networks. Global viewers can catch the anime on Crunchyroll, with the exception of Asia. The creative team behind the series is equally impressive. Ryuichi Kimura, known for works like Kemono Friends 2 and Aikatsu!, is directing the project at BN Pictures. Keiichirō Ōchi, the mastermind behind series like The Quintessential Quintuplets and The Demon Girl Next Door, is overseeing the series composition. Shiori Fujisawa is responsible for character design, while John Kanda and XELIK collaborate on the music composition.
