Goblin Slayer Season 2 Release Date Announced, Premiere on 6th October

The highly anticipated second season of Goblin Slayer has been confirmed to premiere on October 6, 2023. The official announcement was made on the anime’s website along with a striking new key visual, setting the stage for the Fall 2023 anime season. Details regarding the release timing in Japan and the specific TV channels for broadcast have also been unveiled.
Based on the manga of the same name by Kumo Kagyu and illustrated by Noboru Kannatsuki, the anime is now being produced by Liden Films, taking over from the previous studio, White Fox, which animated the first season in 2018.
